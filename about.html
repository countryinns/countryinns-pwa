About Country Inns

Country Inns is a distinguished collection of 7 traditional pubs in the New Forest and surrounding areas. Each pub has its own character, but all share a love of local food, drink, and community.

Our Story

Our establishments are known for their warm hospitality, freshly prepared homemade food, and a fine selection of local real ales. We take pride in sourcing ingredients locally and preparing dishes on-site to ensure the highest quality for our guests.

A Perfect Place to Pause

Ideally situated for those exploring the natural beauty of the New Forest, our pubs are welcoming rest stops for walkers, cyclists, dog owners, and horse riders. Whether you’re after a hearty meal, a refreshing drink, or a comfortable place to stay, you’ll find rustic charm and modern comfort in every Country Inn.

Our Promise

At Country Inns, we are committed to providing exceptional service and creating memorable experiences for all our guests. We invite you to visit any of our locations and discover the unique character and hospitality that define us.
