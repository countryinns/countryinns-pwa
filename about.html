Who We Are

Country Inns is a family of seven pubs in the heart of the New Forest. Each pub has its own charm and personality, but all share the same love of local produce, warm hospitality, and community spirit.

From cosy country inns to vibrant village pubs, our venues are the perfect place to enjoy locally sourced food and drink, celebrate special moments, or simply relax with friends and family.
